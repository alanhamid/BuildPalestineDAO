About Sharia Coin DAO

Sharia Coin DAO is a decentralized financial ecosystem built on the Solana blockchain, designed to empower communities in the MENA region while adhering to Sharia-compliant principles. Our mission is to provide ethical, interest-free financial solutions that drive long-term economic growth and social impact.

Our Purpose

  • Empowering MENA Communities: Through the Sadaqah Jariyah Fund, we support projects that help individuals rebuild their lives, including education, healthcare, and infrastructure.
  • Sharia-Compliant Financial Solutions: Offering an alternative to traditional finance by enabling halal transactions, investments, and governance.
  • Transparent & Decentralized Governance: Token holders shape the future of Sharia Coin DAO through a fair, blockchain-based decision-making system.
  • Sharia Visa Card: A fully halal digital and physical payment solution, integrating Sharia Coin into real-world transactions.

With a commitment to innovation, transparency, and community-driven impact, Sharia Coin DAO is more than a cryptocurrency—it’s a movement toward financial inclusion and ethical prosperity.
